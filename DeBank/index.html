Why DeBank

Built to make you save.

Time-lock discipline

Choose your own unlock date — from one day to five years. Your assets wait patiently, even when you're tempted not to.

Always an exit

Non-custodial vault

How it works

Three steps to a better saving habit.

01

Connect your wallet

Connect MetaMask to OPN Chain testnet. DeBank detects your network and switches automatically — no manual setup.

02

Deposit & choose a date

Pick your asset (IOPN, USDC, USDT), amount, and unlock date. From one week to five years — whatever goal you're working toward.

03

Withdraw when ready

When your date arrives, withdraw the full amount for a small fee. Or exit early anytime — you always stay in control.
